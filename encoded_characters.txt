The following is a list of characters that have been specially encoded in IcePaHC

As XML tag:

…	<ellipsis/>
- – —	<dash/>
%	<percent/>

Separated from adjacent word as CODE:
(	(CODE PR:S) or (CODE <paren>)
)	(CODE PR:E) or (CODE </paren>)

Occasionally left combined with the preceding word (number or letter):
)       <closeparen/>
